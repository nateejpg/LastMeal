What is LastMeal?

LastMeal is a restaurant literally at the end of the universe, where you will experience the best food you could ever have in your entire life. as part of the show, you will see the entire universe passing through your eyes in a spectacular show created by LM production, meanwhile, you will be eating your meal with one of the most delightful experiences that the universe could've ever provided you. Something very important to notice is that whenever you try this food, the answer to life, the universe and everything is part of one of the main ingredients of the food, leading to a very plesurable and unforgetful experience.

Our Menu

Our menu is composed of three meals, all of them as flavourly as they can be, we chose to have only three of them in order to maintain the quality and improve molecularly in the most important points of our food, if you will, try to have all of em, but don't forget about the time, it's almost at it's end ;)

BigBang

An explosion of flavors that resulted in the beginning of everything. The BigBang is a meal for all ages who dont have a heart disease of any kind. It goes with our String Fries and Spinoza's spit as a beverege.

BlackHole

BlackHole is on top tier in our restaurant, a meal capable of making you think if what you know is true, and change your reality. Once you try it for the first time, you will notice that you wont be able to get rid of it, since you'll be at the at the event horizon.

Singularity

Singularity is as the name says, singular. The most unpredictable meal in our restaurant, singularity is a completely different thing everytime the chefs prepare it, so be ready to amuse yourself. It goes with rest of the combo, along with Spinoza's spit and String fries.

Our Chefs

Our chefs came from all over the universe to serve you with their unique food, varying from so many different places that you cannot even image, and probably wouldn't have time for it. Our main chefs are Zaphod, Ford and Marvin, all prepared to blow your mind with their flavorish dishes.

Ford

Ford came from a planet very far away from the end of the universe. His planet was destroyed when he was a kid, and he was picked by a spaceship afterwards, a spaceship where the most renowed chefs work, pizza planet.

Zaphod

Zaphod is an alien who was once the ruler of the Milky Way, but was taken down after his adventures on the Improbability Spaceship. Zaphod learned how to cook with his ex fiance, a woman from earth, or a.k.a the Deep Thought's sucessor, to calculate the answer to life, the universe and everything.

Marvin

Marvin is a robot, his work relies on the most peculiar and scientifically proven ways to cook food, where he studies very hard to create food in the molecular level. Marvin has depression, and the only reason that he found to live was to create food, or should we say, the last meal someone's gonna get.
